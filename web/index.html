4Advanced - foot technique

Hi-Hat Pedal
Adds a left-foot hi-hat “chick” on the 2 & 4 backbeat - the foot-pedal pattern real DTXMania/GITADORA charts use. It only fills beats where your hands aren't already on a hi-hat/cymbal, so nothing doubles up.
DKDK Mode
When ON, kicks too fast to play one-legged are auto-converted to a double kick - right-foot kick + left pedal (DTXMania style). Kicks slow enough for one foot stay as-is.
A human-playability check runs at the end and auto-relaxes anything impossible for 2 hands + 2 feet.
